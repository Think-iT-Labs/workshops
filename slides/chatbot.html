what are chatbots?

In the IT field, a bot is a program that mimic on using functionalities that are designed for users.

In our case the bot will be consuming the chats features.

why chatbots are such a big opportunity?

Increasing amount of messaging apps.

A new way of presenting information.

Get the best of a chatbot

Choose the plateform depending of your audiance
  • Slack: Companies, devs & comunities
  • Telegram: Journalists, activists & comunities
  • IRC: devs (Gurus lives there)
  • Messenger: Lambda users
  • In website Bots: Website visitors
Be the impossible human

Don't repeat your self frequently.

Fulfill your purpose but also answer to random questions.

Be informal, sometimes funny and always polite.

Try to understand everything (AI: NLP).

Be as interactive as the plateform can go.
